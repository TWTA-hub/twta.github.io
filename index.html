About Me

With a background in design and a growing expertise in programming, I bring a multidisciplinary mindset to every project I touch. From visual storytelling to system logic, I thrive at the intersection of beauty, function, and purpose.

Over the years, I've expanded my focus beyond design into product development and digital strategy, driven by the belief that between creation and business, there’s marketing—where ideas meet impact.

I’m passionate about building tools, brands, and experiences that connect deeply with people and solve real-world problems. When I’m not immersed in a project, you’ll find me exploring theology, sketching product ideas, or experimenting with specialty coffee methods.
